RockMyRun is a San Diego based startup that makes intelligent music that synchronizes with your body movements to improve the efficacy of your workout. My work at RockMyRun primarily involved managing the mysql database and the corresponding web content on Joomla and further creating systems to analyze the user data tracked in the database to improve user conversion rates. I also did some user interface work at RockMyRun on the website as well as the mobile app. One such thing I worked on was the myBeat technology(in the left most picture below) that allows you to alter the bpm of a given track whilst running so that you can match your pace with the music.
